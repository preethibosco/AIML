Q6) Write some conclusion on how K-Nearest Neighbors implementation serves as an efficient solution for credit card fraud detection¶

This model is good and simple in predicting non fruad transactionwith 100% in precision,recall and F! score. But even though the fraud detection is good enough its not acceptable given the high financialimpact in missinga fraud transaction. hence methods like CostSensitiveLogisticRegression can be considered for greater presion in detecting fraud transaction. Even though the model was well performing and simple, it eeds improvement in terms of fraud dection percentage.
